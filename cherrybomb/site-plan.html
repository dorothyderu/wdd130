Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be able to move my re-selling business to my own site. I want to be able to provide a website that makes purchasing my product easier and provide complete descriptions. This will allow me to keep all my profits instead of sharing with a third party site.

Audience

My audience is typically going to be fashion-forward women from ages 15-30. They will tend to be on the wealthier side and have an interest in trends. The website needs to look cute and on trend in order for the audience to stay engaged.
